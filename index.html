Oasis

We create unique software and hardware for video production that enables our community of users to produce amazing content.

Our company was founded in 1999 and released the first version of our video production software in 2001. Our products are used by a community of dedicated video producers, sound designers, and artists from across the world.

Creating unique video production software and hardware is not easy. It takes time, effort, and learning. But when you are in the flow, it is incredibly rewarding.

At our company, we share the same passion for what we make and the people we make it for. We strive to create innovative video production tools that enable our users to produce amazing content.

Our video production company comprises over 350 talented individuals from 30 different countries, with offices in Berlin, Los Angeles, and Tokyo.

Most of us are active video producers, directors, and editors, and many of us use our video production tools every day. We come from diverse cultural and professional backgrounds, with some of us having PhDs, some being self-taught, and most of us being somewhere in between.

What connects us is the shared belief that each of us has the skills and knowledge to contribute to something big: helping to shape the future of video production culture.

We believe that creating outstanding video production tools requires focus. We only work on a few products and strive to make them great. Instead of having a one-size-fits-all process, we try to give our people what they need to work their magic and grow.

We have learned that achieving the best results comes from building teams that are richly diverse, and thus able to explore problems from a wider set of perspectives. We donâ€™t always agree with each other, but opinion and debate are valued and openly encouraged.

We are passionate about creating outstanding video production tools and improving ourselves. We strive to foster an environment where people can grow both personally and professionally, and we create a wealth of opportunities to learn from and with each other.

Alongside an internal training program, employees are actively supported in acquiring new knowledge and skills, and coached on applying these in their daily work.

In addition, staff-organized development and video production salons are a chance to discuss new technologies, production techniques and best practices.

We want our employees to love it here. Since we have looking for exceptional talent from around the world, we will do everything we can to make your transition as easy as possible.

If you are joining us, we will help with relocation and paperwork. We will even provide you with free language lessons. Plus, working with us means you can expect comprehensive health insurance for you and your family, as well as generous maternity and paternity leave.

Office hours are flexible, but it is not all work; we have several company and team outings throughout the year as well as a variety of fun, informal small-group activities.

We are really proud of the work we have done so far. But there is so much more to come. If you do like to be a part of it, please join us.
